Well Water

The property has its own well providing fresh, clean water that is regularly tested and safe to drink. Being on an island with limited resources, we kindly ask you to conserve water where possible — take shorter showers, turn off the tap while brushing your teeth, and promptly report any leaks.

🌡️

Heating & Cooling

The house has a heat pump system providing both heating and air conditioning year-round, controlled via remote. The wall radiators are reserved for winter use only.

🔥

Wood Sauna

Use fresh tap water only for creating steam — never seawater. Salt damages the stove and affects its functionality. Firewood is stocked by the green shed and ready for use.

🗑️

Trash & Recycling

The bin is located behind the parking lot and is locked to prevent wildlife from accessing it. Use the blue keychain to unlock it. Proper disposal helps keep the island clean and beautiful. If you have any questions about recycling, feel free to reach out.

🚨

Emergency: 112

Toilet

The Cinderella incineration toilet — eco-friendly and clean. Just follow these steps.

1

Place the filter paper

Before each use, place a filter paper in the bowl — this is essential for the incineration process to work correctly.

2

Use the toilet

For number two with minimal waste, add a couple of cups of water to the filter paper first so the flush works properly.

3

Close lid & press start

Close the lid and press start. Incineration runs automatically — no need to wait.

🌲

Just need to pee?

Going in the forest is perfectly fine — and it helps preserve toilet capacity.

🔧

Stuck filter?

Place a new filter on top, add ~0.5 litres of water, and flush again.
