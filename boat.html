Getting Started

Basic instructions are given at check-in. Keep these in mind while out on the water.

Operating the Boat

  • →Operators must have prior boating experience and basic maritime knowledge.
  • →The archipelago has shallow spots and submerged rocks — always use a navigation app. We recommend Skippo, which is free for basic use.
  • →Observe all speed limits and designated boating areas.

After Use

  • →Return the boat to its original location and secure it properly.
  • →Verify all equipment is accounted for.
  • →Report any damage immediately.

Safety Rules

🦺

Life Jackets — Mandatory

All persons on the motorboat must wear a lifejacket at all times. Life jackets are stored in the dock storage box.

👶

Children

Adult supervision is required for children at all times on the boat.

🚫

Alcohol & Drugs

Operating the boat under the influence of alcohol or drugs is prohibited under Swedish maritime law.

Fuel Policy

  • →5-litre emergency tank on board.
  • →Extra fuel will be supplied if needed.
