Island living

Cinderella Toilet

An incineration toilet — a clean, eco-friendly solution perfectly suited to island life. Just follow these simple steps.

How to Use It

1

Place the filter paper

Before each use, place a filter paper in the bowl. This is crucial for the proper functioning of the incineration process.

2

Use the toilet

For number two: if waste is minimal, add a couple of cups of water to the filter paper first to ensure the flush works properly.

3

Close the lid and press start

After use, close the lid and press the start button. Incineration begins automatically — you don't need to wait around for it to finish.

Tips

🌲

Peeing? Go outside

If you only need to pee, it's perfectly fine — and encouraged — to go in the forest. This helps preserve toilet capacity for when it's really needed.

🔧

Stuck filter?

If the filter gets blocked, simply place a new filter on top of it, add around 0.5 litres of water, and flush again. That usually does the trick.

💬

Questions?

If something seems off or you're unsure about anything, don't hesitate to reach out — we're here to help make your stay comfortable.
